Project Summary:

Overview:
The current process for submitting a thesis in the UA graduate school is deemed inefficient, with a notable emphasis on addressing formatting issues rather than evaluating the substantive content of the theses. To streamline and enhance this process, we have developed a web application mirroring the functionality of an established ASU document generator previously utilized by Dr. Denham. By implementing this solution, we anticipate a marked improvement in the efficiency of operations at the Graduate School. The application aims to minimize the need for manual document checks, as the generated documents adhere to the requisite formatting standards, alleviating the burden of formatting-related challenges and allowing for a more streamlined and effective workflow.
The Thesis and Dissertation Template Generator is an online platform designed to streamline the process of creating academic documents for graduate students. This user-friendly website empowers users to customize and generate personalized templates for their theses or dissertations, ensuring adherence to formatting guidelines and saving valuable time during the document creation process.


Key Features:

    User-Friendly Interface:

  • Intuitive design to provide a seamless experience for users.
  • Easy navigation through a series of 9 steps, ensuring a straightforward process.

    • Step-by-Step Customization:

  • Users go through a structured sequence of steps, providing essential information for template generation.
  • Each step focuses on a specific aspect of the document, such as formatting style, chapter organization, citation style, etc.

    • Customization Options:

  • Multiple template customization options, allowing users to tailor the document according to their program's requirements.
  • Flexibility in choosing font styles, page layouts, and other formatting elements.

    • Guided Content Input:

  • Guidance prompts to assist users in providing necessary information for each section of the document.
  • Suggestions and examples to help users make informed decisions during the customization process.

    • Export Options:

  • Generated Document is exported as a Word Document for the student to edit.
